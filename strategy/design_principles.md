# Design Principles — Before You Bet

*These principles are opinionated. They define not just what the product does, but what it refuses to do.*

---

## 1. Zero friction to value
No accounts, no onboarding, no input. You land, you understand.

## 2. The product leads, the user follows
We don't ask "what do you want to know?" — we tell you what matters.

## 3. Less is more
Curated over exhaustive. 4 sections, not 40. 3 players, not a full roster.

## 4. Plain English only
Every piece of jargon gets translated. If a bettor's mom can't understand it, rewrite it.

## 5. Every section answers "So what?"
No data for data's sake. Every number, every insight earns its place by answering why it matters to you, right now.

## 6. Confident, not reckless
We have a point of view. We share it clearly. But we never promise outcomes.

## 7. Trust through craft
Premium editorial feel. The design itself communicates credibility.

---

## Anti-Principles (What We Refuse)
- No gambling encouragement
- No "guaranteed picks"
- No information overload
- No dark patterns
- No account walls
