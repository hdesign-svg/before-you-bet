Borders

Before You Bet — Border styles, dividers, and separator patterns

Border Colors

Default
neutral-200
Primary border color. Used for dividers, card separators, table rows, and content boundaries.
Subtle
neutral-100
Barely visible borders for internal divisions within cards or panels where a full border feels heavy.
Strong
neutral-300
Higher contrast borders for focused inputs, hover states, or elements that need stronger definition.

Accent Borders

Coral Accent
coral-600 · 3px
Active tab indicator, selected state underline. Always bottom-positioned, 3px thick.
Success
green-500
Valid form fields, success states. Pairs with green-50 background.
Error
red-500
Invalid form fields, error states. Pairs with red-50 background.

Dividers

Section divider 1px solid neutral-200 · Used between slate groups, above footer
Internal divider 1px solid neutral-100 · Used within cards, between intel rows

Widths

1px — Default
Standard border width for all structural borders, dividers, and form fields.
2px — Emphasis
Focus rings, selected card outlines, emphasized boundaries.
3px — Accent
Reserved for brand accents only — active tab indicators and decorative underlines.
